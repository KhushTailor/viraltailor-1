My Story

Passionate about healing through food

I am Viral Tailor, a certified Ayurvedic Nutritionist based in Vadodara, Gujarat. My journey into nutrition began with a deep passion for understanding the relationship between food and health — and how the right diet can truly transform lives.

With 1.5 years of hands-on experience, I have helped 100+ clients across India and internationally achieve their health goals — from sustainable weight loss to reversing thyroid disorders through targeted nutrition plans.

I combine the ancient wisdom of Ayurvedic nutrition with evidence-based modern nutritional science to create plans that are not just effective, but also enjoyable and sustainable for the long term.

My approach is simple: I listen, I understand, and I create a plan made just for you. No generic diets, no shortcuts — just real, lasting results.
